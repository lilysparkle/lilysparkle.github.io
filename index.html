These are some good resources for non-profit organizations in Las Cruces.

Non-profits are so important because they provide resources for everyone, and strengthen the community.


Casa de Peregrinos

Casa de Peregrinos is a non profit organization founded in 1979 in Las Cruces, New Mexico. Their goal is to provide food, shelter, and clothes for the homeless.

Casa de Peregrinos' website

The Boys and Girls Club of Las Cruces

The Boys and Girls Club is a nationwide non profit organisation that helps children grow and become awesome members of society.

Boys and Girls Club's website

Habitat for Humanity ReStore

Habitat for Humanity is a thrift store in Las Cruces, New Mexico, that uses the funds that it gets to build homes for people in the community who need them.

Habitat for Humanity ReStore's website

La Piñon Sexual Assault and Recovery Services

La Piñon is a non-profit organization that gives survivors of sexual assault a place to share their experiences where they will be supported. They have services such as a crisis hotline and counseling.

La Piñon Sexual Assault And Recovery Services' website
